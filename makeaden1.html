Making a Den Making a den can be great fun! Here is some steps on how to do it! STEP 1: Find a tree. It can be any size! STEP 2: Find some big logs and pile them up beside the tree. STEP 3: You will need some friends to help out for this step. Get the big logs and put them in a circle shape, leaning on the tree.

STEP 4: If you want you can decorate your den with leaves and flowers! Have fun! Written by iliketocode and last edited at 16th October 2015
